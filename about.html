About Mkanga-Ulimi Farm

Growing agriculture through passion, learning and sustainability.

Our Story

Mkanga-Ulimi Farm is an agricultural enterprise focused on crop production and livestock farming.

Our ambition is to learn more about agriculture, encourage young people to participate in farming and create value through sustainable agricultural practices.

We believe everyone can be a farmer and contribute towards food security and community development.
