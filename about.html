About Mkanga-Ulimi Farm

Growing quality agricultural products through passion, innovation and sustainable farming.

Our Story

Mkanga-Ulimi Farm is an agricultural business focused on producing quality farm products and providing reliable farming solutions.

We use sustainable farming practices to provide fresh products, support food security and create value for our customers.

Our Mission

To develop successful crop and livestock farming through continuous learning, innovation, and sustainable practices while producing quality agricultural products and sharing knowledge to educate and inspire others, especially young people, to become farmers.

Our Vision

To become a leading and inspiring agricultural brand that promotes sustainable farming, empowers communities, and encourages the next generation to embrace agriculture as a path to growth and opportunity.

Our Values

Quality • Integrity • Sustainability • Innovation • • Passion For Agriculture
